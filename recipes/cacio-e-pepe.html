Pasta cacio e pepe

Description

Creamy cacio e pepe pasta to surprise your family and get you compliments.

Ingredients for 4 people:

  • 400 g of spaghetti pasta
  • 280 g of grated pecorino romano
  • 1 tbs. of black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water for the pasta, add some salt and add the pasta.
  2. Toast the black pepper on a pan at medium heat for a couple of minutes then add 3 ladles of the boiling water from the pot and put it aside.
  3. Put the grated pecorino romano in a bowl and add 2 ladles of boiling water. Mix it until it has a pasty consistency.
  4. 2 minutes before the pasta is cooked, take it and put it in the pan with your pepper, and cook it there for a couple of minutes adding some water when needed.
  5. When the pasta is ready, turn off the heat and let it rest for a minute. Add the pecorino cream with 2 ladles of the boiling water (little by little) until the pasta becomes creamy.
  6. Move the pasta energically so that the cream melts and spreads eavenly. Prepare the portions and complete the dishes adding some more black pepper on top.
  7. Enjoy!
