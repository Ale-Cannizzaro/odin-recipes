Pasta alla carbonara

Description

Delicious and creamy pasta alla carbonara recipe that will get you compliments.

Ingredients for 4 people:

  • 400 g of pasta (spaghetti, mezze maniche)
  • 280 g of guanciale
  • 200 g of grated pecorino romano
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1 whole egg
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water for the pasta.
  2. Cut the rind from the guanciale and chop it to strips
  3. Cook it in the pan at medium heat without oil, it will cook in its fat. Wait until it becomes crispy and put it aside.
  4. Put the egg yolks and the whole egg together in a bowl, add the pecorino romano, mix it up until it becomes creamy and add some black pepper.
  5. When the water is boiling add the pasta and some salt. When it's ready, drain the pasta (while keeping some of the water aside) and put it in the pan.
  6. Add the cream and the guanciale and mix it up.
  7. If the cream is too dense add some of the boiled water you put aside until it becomes creamy.
  8. Add some black pepper if you like and mix.
  9. Enjoy!
