Lasagne alla bolognese

Description

Delicious Lasagne alla bolognese recipe for a great family launch.

Ingredients

Pasta:

  • 3 fresh eggs
  • 300 g of flour
  • a pinch of salt

Ragú:

  • 700 g of mixed minced meat
  • 70 g of celery
  • 180 g of onions
  • 100 g of carrots
  • 400 g of tomato puree
  • a sprig of rosemary
  • a glass of white wine
  • 20 g of extra virgin olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Plus:

  • 600 ml of bechamel
  • 150 g of grated parmesan cheese
  • 40 g of butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the pastry with flour, eggs and a pinch of salt. Knead it and let it rest for 30 minutes protected.
  2. Finely chop the vegetables and brown them in oil with the rosemary
  3. Add the minced and browned meat, then add the wine and let it evaporate
  4. Add the tomato puree and continue cooking at low heat for 80 minutes, adding some water if needed. Then add salt and pepper.
  5. Roll out the dough thinly, cut it into rectangles and dip them for a few moments in boiling salted water, then dain and dry them on a cloth
  6. Brush a 26x20 cm baking mold with 20 g of melted butter and line the bottom with the pastry rectangles, then spread a ragú layer.
  7. Cover it with bechamel
  8. Add some grated parmesan cheese. Make a new layer with pasta, ragú and bechamel, then add some grated parmesan cheese again.
  9. Repeat the operation until you run out of the ingredients
  10. Spread some cheese and some butter flakes on the surface
  11. Place the lasagne in the oven at 190° for 40 minutes.
  12. Enjoy!
